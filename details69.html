RUG 1: HIDING

You are Christian.
Your neighbors are Muslim.
You look sadly at each other and shake your heads in whispered conversations.
This isn’t right.
You hide each other.
When extremists come knocking, you swear on their holy books you aren’t hiding the enemy.
You lie.
God smiles.
