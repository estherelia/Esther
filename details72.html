LIVE DRAWING AT THE IRANIAN DIASPORA CONFERENCE

I was privileged to attend the first ever Iranian Diaspora Conference hosted by the Center for Iranian Diaspora Studies in San Francisco. The speakers and content were so striking, I couldn't help but record some of the contemporary scholars researching and recording the complexities of the changing culture of a people forced to live outside their homeland.
