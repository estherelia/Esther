NANAJAN & BUELAH

A portrait of my great-grandmother Nanajan and her daughter Buelah as a chair/ottoman set.
When fleeing their homeland, raids and the need to escape quickly caused many of my ancestor's possessions to be lost to us forever. This set tells the story of the journey out of Iran, and how Nanajan gave birth to Buelah during the journey out of Iran.

2019
16x18x36, 15x10x15 inches
Poplar, Acrylic
